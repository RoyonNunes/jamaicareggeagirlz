About the Reggae Girlz

The Jamaica women's national football team, also known as the Reggae Girlz, has a storied history in international football. The team made history by becoming the first Caribbean country to qualify for the FIFA Women's World Cup in 2019.

Key Achievements

  • 2019 FIFA Women's World Cup Qualifiers
  • 2023 FIFA Women's World Cup Qualifiers
  • CONCACAF Women's Championship Appearances
