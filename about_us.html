About the Reggae Girlz

The Jamaica women's national football team, affectionately known as the Reggae Girlz, has made waves in international football. They made history by becoming the first Caribbean team to qualify for the FIFA Women's World Cup in 2019, showcasing their talent and determination on the global stage.

Supporters of the Reggae Girlz

The Reggae Girlz have garnered immense support from fans, celebrities, and influential figures. Here are some of the key supporters who have stood by the team:
